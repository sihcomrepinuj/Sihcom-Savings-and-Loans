How it works

  1. Build a savings balance first.
  2. Request a draw of any amount up to your total savings.
  3. Once disbursed, the drawn amount is frozen out of your interest-earning balance.
  4. Repay by sending ISK in-game; payments auto-apply to the loan first, then to savings.
  5. When the balance hits zero, your full savings starts earning interest again.

Matches your savings rate exactly. The frozen-collateral portion of your savings stops earning interest, so the net cost is the spread on the unfrozen portion only.

Rules

  • Maximum draw is your current total savings balance.
  • One active loan per pilot — opening a credit line blocks general loans and vice versa.
  • Withdrawal, cancellation, and goal-funding actions are blocked while a credit line is open.
  • No liquidation: your savings stays yours; the draw simply freezes the matching portion.
