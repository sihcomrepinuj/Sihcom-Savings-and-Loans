How Interest Works

Interest compounds on each member's full savings balance every period.

Frozen collateral: If a member has an open credit line, the outstanding balance is treated as frozen collateral and does not earn savings interest until the credit line is paid off. General loans do not freeze savings.

Example: For 10% monthly interest with daily compounding, set period to Daily and rate to 0.003333 (10% ÷ 30 days). A 1B ISK deposit earns ~3.3M ISK/day, starting immediately.
